Designing technology for environmental data

Environmental data management is far behind where it needs to be. Lack of adequate tools and support are holding back progress on environmental issues. To pull environmental monitoring forward we need easy and usable data management systems in place. And that's where Capra comes in.

So, how does your organization handle the collection, management, and sharing of your environmental data?

Let us help

We build gorgeous, custom digital solutions for the collection, management and sharing of environmental data. Water quality is our specialty, but we do everything. And by the way, we also develop the data collection kits to collect parameters in the field.

Capra is a solution to very specific issues that our founders recognized and addressed in their environmental science work. Designing environmental monitoring kits, developing open data standards, establishing successful monitoring programs, and building data sharing platforms used by millions of people are just a few examples of the solutions we've executed.

Water Rangers

Water Rangers is a unique and cutting edge tool set that  not only encourages communities to collect strong, relevant, usable data, but also makes it easier and more joyful to do so. The team at Capra built an in-field data collection app, in-house data management tools, and a world class data sharing platform. Already 140 groups are actively testing in Canada, United States, Mexico, and more!
